Title: Qualifying Examinations Record Page
Source URL: https://www.rgcet.edu.in/qe.php

Official page summary:
- Students qualifying in state, national, or international level examinations during the last five years
- Examples listed on the page include IIT JAM, NET, SLET, GATE, GMAT, GPAT, CLAT, CAT, GRE, TOEFL, IELTS, Civil Services, and State Government examinations
- Years shown on the page: 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22, 2022-23

The page provides download links for last five years student name lists and year-wise qualifying examination records.
