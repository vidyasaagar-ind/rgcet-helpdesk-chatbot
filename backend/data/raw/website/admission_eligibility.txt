Title: Eligibility for Admission
Source URL: https://www.rgcet.edu.in/pages-admission.php

Admission inquiry note on the page:
- 2026-27 Admission Open

First semester B.Tech degree programme eligibility:
- Passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
- One of Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology along with the compulsory subjects
- At least 45 percent marks in the above subjects taken together
- Reserved category candidates: 40 percent in the above subjects taken together

Lateral entry into third semester of the eight semester B.Tech programme:
- Diploma examination from an AICTE approved institution or DOTE approved with at least 50 percent marks
- Reserved category candidates: 45 percent
- B.Sc. degree from a recognized university defined by UGC with at least 50 percent marks and XII standard with Mathematics
- Reserved category candidates: 45 percent
- B.Sc. stream students shall clear Engineering Graphics / Engineering Drawing and Engineering Mechanics of the first year engineering programme along with second year subjects
- B.Sc. stream students are considered after filling the supernumerary seats in this category with students from the Diploma stream
- Students who have passed Diploma in Engineering and Technology or B.Sc. degree are also eligible for first year Engineering Degree courses subject to vacancies if lateral entry vacancies are exhausted

MCA eligibility:
- Recognized Bachelor's Degree of minimum 3 years duration
- Mathematics at 10+2 level
- At least 50 percent at the qualifying examination
- Reserved category candidates: 45 percent

MBA eligibility:
- Recognized Bachelor's Degree of minimum 3 years duration
- At least 50 percent at the qualifying examination
- Reserved category candidates: 45 percent

Age limit details on the page:
- Candidates should not have completed 21 years of age as on 1st July of the academic year under consideration
- Lateral entry candidates should not have completed 24 years as on 1st July of the academic year under consideration
- SC/ST candidates have a relaxation of 3 years in both cases
- MCA: No age limit
- MBA: No age limit
