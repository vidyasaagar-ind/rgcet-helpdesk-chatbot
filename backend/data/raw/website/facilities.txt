Title: Facilities For Students
Source URL: https://www.rgcet.edu.in/pages-facilities.php

Students Grievance Cell:
- The grievance cell looks into student grievances
- Students may also express grievances online through the grievance form mentioned on the page

Health Service:
- The college doctor will be available in the college campus for consultation
- Students can seek advice from the Medical Officer any time
- Regular health check-ups will be conducted for students
- Students can consult the college doctor even after college hours at his chamber
- Hospital services are available at Mahatma Gandhi Medical College and Research Institute nearby the campus

Transport:
- The college has a fleet of 18 buses
- Buses transport students and staff from various parts of the city and neighbouring areas to the college and back home
- College bus facility is available to every student
- The facility is extended for special coaching classes, placements and training, and campus drive occasions

Internet Service:
- Member of the National Knowledge Network (NKN)
- NKN bandwidth: 40 Mbps
- Additional BSNL internet connection: 100 Mbps
- Network traffic is monitored and controlled through a firewall
- A fiber-optic backbone has been laid to provide connectivity to various parts of the campus

WiFi Connectivity:
- Access points have been deployed in a few blocks in the campus

Hostel Facilities:
- The institution provides hostel facilities for boys and girls
- Vegetarian food is provided
- Lunch is provided inside the campus for all students
- A separate hostel building with One Lakh Sq. ft. is described as being constructed and ready for occupation in the near future
