Title: Centre for Training Placement and Corporate Relations
Source URL: https://www.rgcet.edu.in/placementcell_about.php

Vision:
- To empower students with industry-relevant skills, professional competence, and ethical values through comprehensive training and placement opportunities, fostering global career success and contributing to societal development

Mission points on the page:
- Build strong industry-academic collaborations for placement and internship opportunities
- Provide personalized career counseling, soft skills training, and technical skill development programmes
- Nurture leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities
- Promote lifelong learning, adaptability, and innovation
- Ensure equal placement opportunities while upholding ethical and professional standards

About the Centre for Training, Placement, and Corporate Relations (CTPCR):
- Serves as a bridge between academia and industry
- Organizes training programmes such as workshops, seminars, and certification courses
- Facilitates campus placements
- Supports industry collaboration, internships, and live projects
- Provides career counseling
- Organizes aptitude and interview preparation
- Promotes higher education guidance
- Supports entrepreneurship
- Encourages alumni engagement
- Tracks industry trends
- Conducts corporate outreach
